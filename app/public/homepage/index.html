What are ChickTech Career Events?

As the tech scene continues to grow, more and more opportunities for both current and future professionals are added every day. ChickTech Career Events are designed to help women professionals network, grow their skills, explore new parts of the tech industry, and discover employers looking for exceptional talent.

Career Meetup Host and Volunteer Opportunities!
